Hummus

Description

Hummus is a delicious and creamy Middle Eastern dip made from blended chickpeas, tahini, olive oil, lemon juice, and garlic. It is often enjoyed as a snack or appetizer, served with pita bread, vegetables, or as a spread in sandwiches. Hummus is not only flavorful but also packed with nutrients, making it a popular choice for those seeking a healthy and satisfying option.

Ingredients

  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1/4 cup tahini
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. In a food processor, combine the chickpeas, tahini, olive oil, lemon juice and garlic.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy. If the mixture is too thick, you can add a little water or more olive oil to achieve your desired consistency.
  3. Season with salt to taste and blend again to incorporate.
  4. Transfer the hummus to a serving bowl and drizzle with additional olive oil if desired.
  5. Serve with pita bread, fresh vegetables, or as a spread in sandwiches. Enjoy!
